Heating

🔥 Good: Reliable & Cost-Effective Heating

Rheem’s “Good” furnaces provide dependable performance at an affordable price. These systems are built for homeowners who want reliable heat without advanced features.

Key Features:

  • Single-stage heating (on/off operation)

  • 80–92% AFUE efficiency options

  • Durable, proven components

  • Lower upfront investment

  • Straightforward operation and maintenance

Best For:

Smaller homes, rental properties, or homeowners looking for dependable heating at the lowest cost.

🔥 Better: Enhanced Comfort & Efficiency

The “Better” tier offers improved comfort, quieter operation, and higher efficiency. These furnaces adapt better to changing temperatures and provide more consistent heat throughout your home.

Key Features:

  • Two-stage heating for better temperature control

  • Up to 96% AFUE efficiency

  • Variable-speed or ECM blower motors

  • Quieter performance

  • Improved airflow and comfort

  • Optional smart thermostat compatibility

Best For:

Most homeowners who want a balance of efficiency, comfort, and long-term value.

🔥 Best: Premium Performance & Maximum Comfort

Rheem’s top-tier furnaces deliver the highest level of comfort, efficiency, and technology. These systems adjust continuously to your home’s heating needs for precise temperature control and energy savings.

Key Features:

  • Modulating gas valve for precise heat output

  • Up to 98% AFUE efficiency

  • Variable-speed blower motor

  • Extremely quiet operation

  • Superior comfort and airflow control

  • Full EcoNet® smart home integration

Best For:

Larger homes, colder climates, or homeowners seeking the most advanced comfort and energy savings available.
