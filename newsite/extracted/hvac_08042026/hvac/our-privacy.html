Our Privacy

HVAC Only Inc is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Information We Collect

We collect personal information you provide to us, including your name, address, and phone number, when you fill out forms on our website or communicate with us directly.

How We Use Your Information

The personal information we collect is used solely to schedule and complete work orders, including communication related to appointments, project updates, and billing

SMS Communications

If you opt in to receive SMS messages from us, your phone number will be used exclusively for communications related to your service. SMS consent is not shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing purposes.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties. Your information is used only as necessary to provide the services you request.

Data Security

We implement appropriate measures to protect your information from unauthorized access or disclosure.

SMS Terms of Service

By opting in to receive SMS communications from HVAC Only Inc through our website or other channels, you consent to receive text messages regarding appointment scheduling, reminders, project updates, and billing notifications. Message frequency may vary. Message and data rates may apply.
