About Us

Our company was built on a shared commitment to quality workmanship, honest service, and keeping people comfortable year-round. Co-owned by Darin Johnson and Evan Johnson, we bring together deep industry roots, technical expertise, and real-world experience to serve our customers with confidence and care.

A Legacy in the HVAC Industry

HVAC runs in the family. Darin grew up around the trade—his father worked in the HVAC industry for over 40 years, instilling a strong work ethic and respect for doing things the right way. That foundation shaped Darin’s understanding of the business side of HVAC and the importance of accuracy, reliability, and professionalism.

Today, Darin handles estimating and detailed reporting, working closely with law firms and contractors. With a background in the storm and restoration industry, he brings valuable experience in inspections, documentation, and navigating complex projects where precision and accountability matter most.

From Caring for People to Comfort Systems

Evan’s path into HVAC is rooted in caring for others. While in high school, Evan was interested in nursing, drawn to helping people feel better. Over time, he realized he could apply that same passion—keeping people comfortable and safe—to the HVAC trade.

Evan began his career working locally in Denver for a large national HVAC company, where he gained hands-on experience across residential and commercial systems. After five years in the field, he knew it was time to build something of his own—something focused on craftsmanship, customer service, and long-term solutions.

Evan quickly earned his Master Mechanical License, EPA Certification, and NATE Certifications, ensuring the highest level of technical knowledge and compliance with industry standards.

Built on Experience, Driven by Integrity

Together, Darin and Evan combine generations of industry insight, modern technical expertise, and a customer-first mindset. Whether it’s a routine service call, a complex mechanical project, or detailed reporting for contractors and legal teams, our goal is always the same: to deliver dependable comfort solutions you can trust.
