Rheem Packaged HVAC Units: Good, Better & Best

Rheem packaged units combine heating and cooling into one all-in-one system. These systems are ideal for homes and light commercial buildings where indoor space is limited or a rooftop or ground-level installation is preferred. Rheem offers packaged units in Good, Better, and Best options to fit different comfort needs and budgets.

❄️Good: Simple, Dependable All-In-One Comfort

Rheem’s “Good” packaged units provide reliable heating and cooling with straightforward operation. These systems are built for durability and consistent performance at an affordable price point.

Key Features:

  • Single-stage heating and cooling

  • Standard efficiency ratings

  • Durable cabinet design for outdoor installation

  • Proven Rheem components

  • Lower upfront cost

  • Easy maintenance access

Best For:

Smaller homes, rental properties, or homeowners looking for a cost-effective packaged solution.

❄️ Better: Improved Efficiency & Comfort Control

The “Better” tier offers enhanced comfort and efficiency compared to entry-level models. These systems run more consistently and quietly, providing better temperature balance throughout the home.

Key Features:

  • Two-stage heating and/or cooling

  • Improved energy efficiency ratings

  • ECM or variable-speed blower motors

  • Quieter operation

  • Better airflow and humidity control

  • Optional smart thermostat compatibility

Best For:

Homeowners who want better comfort, quieter operation, and improved efficiency without stepping into premium pricing.

Best: Advanced Performance & Maximum Efficiency

Rheem’s top-tier packaged units deliver superior comfort, energy savings, and advanced technology. These systems automatically adjust output to match your home’s needs, maximizing comfort in all seasons.

Key Features:

  • Multi-stage or modulating heating and cooling

  • High SEER / EER cooling performance

  • Variable-speed blower motors

  • Advanced humidity control

  • Extremely quiet operation

  • EcoNet® smart system compatibility

  • Premium cabinet and corrosion-resistant design

Best For:

Larger homes, demanding comfort requirements, or homeowners seeking the most advanced packaged HVAC solution available
